To keep multiple backups, you should use Timestamps or Phase Markers. This allows you to go back to "Phase 4" or "Phase 5" specifically if a surgical injection fails.

The Versioning Protocol
Run these in your terminal depending on how you want to organize:

Option A: The Phase Marker (Best for Milestones)

Bash

cp app.js app.phase5.backup.js
Option B: The Date/Time Stamp (Best for Daily Work)

Bash

# For December 25th
cp app.js app.dec25.backup.js
How to "Roll Back" (The Emergency Switch)
If you perform an injection and the screen goes black or the terminal throws a "Syntax Error," do not panic. Simply "Restore from Backup" by running:

Bash

# This replaces the broken app.js with your clean backup
cp app.phase5.backup.js app.js
